2020 Data Analysis of Takeaway Users

After the Covid-19, the scale of online food delivery users rebounded. As of March 2020, the number of online food delivery users in China reached 39.78 million, and the utilization rate of food delivery users reached 44.0%; among them, the number of online food delivery users on mobile phones reached 396.53 million, and the utilization rate reached 44.2%. Affected by the epidemic, the number of online food delivery users in the first quarter of 2020 has decreased compared with the end of 2018. With the normalization of domestic epidemic prevention and control, the catering industry has resumed work and consumption as a whole, the average daily order volume of food has gradually recovered, and the scale of food delivery users is expected to pick up.
